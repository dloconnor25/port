USER INTERFACE & USER EXPERIENCE

In the summer of 2016 I was living across the country, pursing my passion for the outdoors. Working as a digital marketing coordinator for an outdoors outfitter, I was constantly dealing with website errors and formatting new content. Knowing nothing about the language of code, I was constantly trouble shooting by googling and "winging it". My inner creative perfectionist couldn't be contained and my desire to create engaging and user-friendly design took over. I knew the next step was to find a way to learn code, so I could acheive what I wanted.

Once I stumbled upon The Iron Yard, my life changed. Quickly I fell in love with HTML and CSS, along with the supportive coding community. Not only was I able to learn a wide range of coding techniques, but I was also able to more deeply refine my fundamental design skills, finding a new passion for logo design.
