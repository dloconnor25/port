I AM DEIRDRE O'CONNOR

If I had to choose one word to describe myself, it would be adventurer. I love trying new things, pushing myself past my limit, and in doing so discovering more about myself. I'm an avid traveler (17 countries and counting), and love free falling, as you can see from my photo. I find pleasure and comfort in drawing endless doodles, listening to The Avett Brothers, and hiking the beautiful Appalachian Mountains.

I am a graduate of both The Iron Yard UI Design school and Appalachian State University. I earned my BS in communication advertising, minoring in studio art. I spent part of my senior year studying abroad in Wellington, New Zealand. Through my studies and travels I've learned about who I am, and what I want out of life. I am a confident, ambitious individual who thrives in new and challenging situations.
